Diabetes data Shape
___________________________________
HighBP – High blood pressure (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
HighChol – High cholesterol (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
CholCheck – Cholesterol checked in the last 5 years (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
BMI – Body Mass Index (numeric)
Smoker – Smoked at least 100 cigarettes (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
Stroke – Ever had a stroke (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
HeartDiseaseorAttack – History of heart disease or heart attack (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
PhysActivity – Physical activity in the last 30 days (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
Fruits – Eats fruits at least once per day (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
Veggies – Eats vegetables at least once per day (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
HvyAlcoholConsump – Heavy alcohol consumption (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
AnyHealthcare – Has any kind of healthcare coverage (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
NoDocbcCost – Could not see a doctor due to cost (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
GenHlth – General health (Scale 1-5, where 1 = Excellent, 5 = Poor)
MentHlth – Number of days mental health was not good in the last 30 days (numeric)
PhysHlth – Number of days physical health was not good in the last 30 days (numeric)
DiffWalk – Difficulty walking or climbing stairs (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
Sex – 1 = Female, 0 = Male
Age – Age group (numeric encoding, e.g., 1 = 18-24, 2 = 25-29, etc.)
Education – Education level (numeric encoding, e.g., 1 = No schooling, 2 = High school, etc.)
Income – Income level (numeric encoding, e.g., 1 = <$10k, 2 = $10k-$20k, etc.)
